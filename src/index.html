What the heck is Droid?

Our Mission

The Ashoka Robotics Society, DROID ROBOTICS\' overarching mission is to establish a vibrant and interdisciplinary ecosystem where Ashokans (students, staff, labs, profs.) can engage in exploratory learning, hone their skills en-route of developing novel solutions to tackle daily life challenges using robotics.

Our Departments

The Projects, and Publications are two pillars upon which the society stands. While the Projects dept. focuses on implementing pragmatic projects, and workshops organisation, facilitating the translation of theory, and prototyping into practicality, the publications engages in dialogues of robotics in Philosophy, Politics, Science etc.

Our Vision

By providing a platform for collaborative projects and workshops, DROID ROBOTICS renders robotics accessible and captivating for Ashoka community from diverse backgrounds, thereby bridging the gap between theoretical concepts and practical implementation in a liberal arts environment.
